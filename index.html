Blood Pressure of SS_HS Rats

Writeup

What have you done so far?

We chose a rat blood pressure dataset.
We processed the raw text files into JSON.
We downsampled the data to 5Hz.
We made an interactive line chart using D3.js.

What will be the most challenging part of your project to design and why?

Adding group switching will be hard.
Too many lines make the chart messy.
Keeping the chart clean needs careful layout.
Also, some rats have noisy data.
